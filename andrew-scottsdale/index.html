About Dr. Andrew Kellogg

Dr. Andrew Kellogg is the founder and lead chiropractor at the Posture & Scoliosis Institute in Sarasota, bringing extensive expertise in spinal health and corrective care to every patient interaction.

"My mission is to help patients achieve optimal spinal health through evidence-based approaches tailored to their unique needs, with a special focus on posture correction and scoliosis management."

With over 7 years of experience co-owning a successful Functional Wellness Center, Dr. Kellogg has developed specialized techniques for addressing complex spinal conditions. His commitment to continued education ensures patients receive the most current and effective treatment options available.

The Posture & Scoliosis Institute was founded on the principle that proper spinal alignment is fundamental to overall health and well-being. Dr. Kellogg's comprehensive approach addresses not just symptoms, but the underlying causes of spinal dysfunction.
