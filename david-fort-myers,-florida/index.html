About Dr. Steiner

Experience & Expertise

Dr. David Steiner has been serving the Fort Myers community for over 22 years with compassionate, results-driven chiropractic care. His approach combines traditional chiropractic techniques with modern physiotherapy to create personalized treatment plans that address the root cause of your pain.

Through his extensive experience working with patients of all ages and conditions, Dr. Steiner has developed a reputation for his thorough evaluations, gentle adjustments, and commitment to patient education. He believes that informed patients achieve better outcomes and is dedicated to empowering every individual who walks through his door.
