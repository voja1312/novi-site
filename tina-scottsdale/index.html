Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of chiropractic treatments tailored to your specific needs, helping you achieve long-lasting pain relief and improved overall wellness.

Chiropractic Adjustments

Gentle spinal manipulations to improve alignment, reduce pain, and enhance nervous system function for better overall health.

Spinal Decompression

Non-surgical treatment that gently stretches the spine to alleviate back pain, neck pain, and symptoms caused by herniated discs.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Tailored rehabilitation programs to help athletes recover from injuries and improve performance through proper healing and strength-building.

Soft Tissue Therapy

Therapeutic massage and myofascial release techniques to reduce muscle tension, improve circulation, and accelerate healing.

Electrical Stimulation

Advanced therapy using mild electrical currents to reduce pain, stimulate healing, and improve muscle function in injured areas.

Corrective Exercises

Personalized exercise programs designed to strengthen weak areas, improve posture, and prevent future injuries.

About Your Chiropractor

Dr. Tina Habhab, DC

Dr. Tina Habhab is a dedicated and experienced chiropractor serving the Scottsdale community. With a passion for helping patients achieve optimal health and wellness, Dr. Habhab combines her extensive knowledge of chiropractic care with a compassionate approach to treatment.

After graduating with honors from her chiropractic program, Dr. Habhab has continuously expanded her expertise through advanced training in various chiropractic techniques and rehabilitation methods. Her comprehensive approach focuses on not just alleviating symptoms but addressing the root causes of pain and discomfort.

Dr. Habhab believes in creating personalized treatment plans that consider each patient's unique needs, health history, and wellness goals. Whether you're recovering from an injury, seeking relief from chronic pain, or wanting to improve your overall health, Dr. Habhab is committed to providing exceptional care in a warm and welcoming environment.
